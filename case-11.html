Colors

Primary Colours

The first primary colours consist of Cool Mint, a light and friendly blue. For more accessibility, one tint and one shade drawn from Cool Mint are also available for use in similar applications. The other primary colours are Ink, which is used for text. White and Black are also primary colours that are used as a base for all of our other colours.

Secondary Colours

The secondary colours can be found in the leaf icon of our logo. On the left, our brighter Spring Green. On the right, our stronger and more mature Jade. These two colours come with two shades for more accessibility. be used minimally. The secondary colours are highlights or accents, and should be used minimally.

Strategy

What’s missing in this competitive real estate market? How do we want people to visualize our brand? Wich tools and platforms will we need to reach the right people? To start things of this, and a ton of other questions had to be answered. We went for a ground-up approach in strategy to go for uwaankoop/uwverkoop – makelaars. Starting with why they offer the service and ending with what defines good service.

Solution

People choose for people not companies. This they stand for. But on the other hand they needed an agency to develop a platform and a strategy which aligns with their high ambitions to take over the market in the coming couple of years. This middle ground is where we found the sweet spot. We took their company philosophy and mixed it with what we think the end-user so deeply desires in a housing market where the demand extremely high.

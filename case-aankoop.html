Objective

What’s missing in this competitive real estate market? How do we want people to visualize our brand? Wich tools and platforms will we need to reach the right people? To start things of this, and a ton of other questions had to be answered. We went for a ground-up approach in strategy to go for uwaankoop/uwverkoop – makelaars. Starting with why they offer the service and ending with what defines good service.

People choose for people not companies. This they stand for. But on the other hand they needed an agency to develop a platform and a strategy which aligns with their high ambitions to take over the market in the coming couple of years. This middle ground is where we found the sweet spot. We took their company philosophy and mixed it with what we think the end-user so deeply desires in a housing market where the demand extremely high.

Custom
calendar tool

To create a better experience for the customer. And gain a better understanding of what they are searching for. We completely custom designed & coded a quiz and integrated this with a calendar tool.

  • Popup CTA

    Users can find this popup tool by clicking on CTA buttons on the website or by clicking the floating calendar icon wich says '' make an appointment'

  • Integrated calender

    Very challenging but we managed to integrate a 3th party calender tool, wich makes it possible to directy make an appointment with your real-estate advisor.

  • Quiz Form

    We integrated a full quiz form, wich steers you deeper in the funnel of wich service you should be offered and wich appointment fits your needs.

  • Better experience

    All in all this popup creates a better experience for the user. We know more about them before the consult and they funnel directly into the right service
